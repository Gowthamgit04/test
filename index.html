The Medical Informatics Lab at the School of Medical Science and Technology, IIT Kharagpur, led by Debashree Guha, focuses on developing intelligent, uncertainty-aware decision-making frameworks for healthcare and biomedical applications. The group integrates decision science, data aggregation, fuzzy logic, and information fusion to address complex clinical and biomedical problems. Emphasis is placed on building interpretable, scalable, and computationally efficient decision support tools that transform heterogeneous and uncertain data into actionable knowledge.
