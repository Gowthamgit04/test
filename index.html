PMML — School of Medical Science and Technology, IIT Kharagpur

The Predictive Mathematical Modelling Lab (PMML) at the School of Medical Science and Technology, IIT Kharagpur, focuses on developing intelligent, uncertainty-aware decision-making frameworks for healthcare and biomedical applications. The group integrates decision science, data aggregation, fuzzy logic, and information fusion to address complex clinical and biomedical problems. Emphasis is placed on building interpretable, scalable, and computationally efficient decision support tools that transform heterogeneous and uncertain data into actionable knowledge. The lab's work supports real-world deployment through web-based diagnostic platforms, rule-based reasoning systems, and physically grounded modeling approaches, with a strong orientation toward translational impact, interdisciplinary collaboration, and technology-driven healthcare solutions.

Dr. Debashree Guha Adhya serves as Associate Professor at the School of Medical Science and Technology (SMST), Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur, where she leads research at the intersection of mathematical decision science and healthcare. Her work spans qualitative and quantitative decision making, medical decision making, fuzzy logic, uncertainty modelling, and data aggregation with applications in medical systems. She develops novel information fusion strategies by modelling interaction among data, analyzes information measures to extract knowledge from uncertain datasets, and builds efficient decision support systems for healthcare development. Dr. Guha Adhya has published extensively in leading journals including IEEE Transactions on Fuzzy Systems and IEEE Transactions on Systems, Man, and Cybernetics.
